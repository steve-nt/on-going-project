HISTORICAL CONTEXT
================================================================================

MAJOR BREACHES & INCIDENTS:

[LinkedIn-2012] LinkedIn data breach (2012)
                6.5 million SHA-1 hashed passwords (unsalted) leaked
                Demonstrated importance of salting

[Adobe-2013] Adobe data breach (2013)
             153 million passwords encrypted (not hashed) with same key
             Showed weakness of encryption vs hashing for passwords

[Yahoo-2013] Yahoo data breach (2013-2014)
             3 billion accounts compromised
             Emphasized need for MFA

[Equifax-2017] Equifax data breach (2017)
               147 million records compromised
               Led to increased security standards

TIMING ATTACK INCIDENTS:

[OpenSSL-2003] OpenSSL timing vulnerability in RSA decryption
               CVE-2003-0147
               Demonstrated real-world timing attack feasibility

[SSH-2001] Song, D., Wagner, D., & Tian, X.
           "Timing Analysis of Keystrokes and Timing Attacks on SSH"
           USENIX Security Symposium, 2001
